    Summary

    Nailivic Studios creates folk art in a variety of sizes, colors, and shapes, all cut from computer controlled lasers. This service manages inventory of parts and items, and calculates production queue. Projections are obtained by importing historical sales data from Square. SKU system established for all possible combinations of size, type, and colors, which is unified across Square, Shopify, and this app.

    Projections are imported directly from Square sales reports, and stored in a database table, which may also be exported again as a csv after modifications. Parts and items inventory database tables can also be imported or exported via csv. Inventory is unified, while projections are stored by event.

    Summary

    Because data connections are often spotty, this delivers a timely weather report to the pilot user through simlple, low data SMS protocols.

    While the client side is handled by the Twilio API, the server parses requests using regular expressions, fetches recent METARs from the Aviation Weather Center Text Data Server, and delivers raw METAR text for any requested K*** airport code.
